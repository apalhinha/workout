Calf raises

Raising your heels is an easy yet effective exercise for the calves

Video demonstration:

Exterpt from Exercise Library: Calf Raises by Darebee

Step by step:

  1. Stand up straight, eventually holding to something
  2. Raise your heels until you are standing on your toes
  3. Bring down the heels back to the floor
  4. This is one repetition

Hints & tips:

  • You can hold on a chair or wall for balance
  • Grabbing some weight or dumbbells will make the exercise more effective
  • Calf raises can easily be done while in the morning mirror or in front of a TV

Benefits:

  • Strong calves stabilize your feet and ankle
  • Lower body performance frequently depends on these muscles, the week link
  • Advantage or explosiveness in sprinting
  • Protect knees on jumps, burpees and any impact actions

Counting & gamification:

  • Count moves along the day
  • Set a goal for 20 consecutive days, restart counting the days if the daily goal is missed
