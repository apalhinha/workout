Close grip push-ups

The exercise is focused on triceps, although chest, core and shoulders are also involved.

Other names: Diamond Push-up

Video demonstration:

Exterpt from 'Exercise Library: Push-Ups / Close Grip' by Darebee

Step by step:

  1. Get down on all fours.
  2. Place hands forming a diamond with thumb and indicator fingers.
  3. Straighten arms and legs.
  4. Lower the body, the chest nearly touches the floor.
  5. Pause, then straight your arms and push back up.

Hints & tips:

  • Triceps strength is needed.
  • Start with wall-triceps and knee-triceps push-ups, if needed.
  • keep your core engaged, the all body in a straight line.

Benefits:

  • Strengthen the triceps.
  • Also works the core, shoulders and chest.

Counting & gamification:

  • Make series of 10.
  • include this in your dailly push-up variations.
