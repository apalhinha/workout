About

Hi. I spend 30+ years without any exercise. Still young, I setup my own business. From there all priorities became mixed up: Countless hours on a desk or phone, fast food, working weekends, caffeine and cigarettes along the night. My own company’s growth kept me moving. In Feb'18 I had to run for a bus. Fancy shoes, nicer trousers, laptop on my back. 40 meters later my lungs were about to explode.
